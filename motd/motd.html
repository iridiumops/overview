Iridium Overview

Simple yet powerful overview pack for everyone,
every situation and all play-styles.
Designed with multiple window layouts in mind,
modular and easily configurable.

Features:
- 12 preconfigured tabs
- 38 overview filters
- 3 bracket filters
- 3 dscan filters
- readable ship labels
- standard color-coding
